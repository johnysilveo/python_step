About us

About ChronoTravel Ltd.

Founded in the golden age of the Hegemony, ChronoTravel Ltd. has guided countless brave (and occasionally reluctant) souls on their journeys to the legendary Time Tombs. While many firms promise “safe passage,” we prefer to offer something far more valuable: memorable experiences.

Our operations combine cutting-edge WorldWeb logistics with the timeless art of pilgrim storytelling. Each tour is designed not only to bring you to the Tombs, but to ensure you return with a tale worthy of the Cantos… provided you return at all.

What We Offer
  • • Expert guides licensed in both navigation and poetry recitation
  • • Climate-controlled sand-skimmers with holo-windows
  • • Complimentary chrono-insurance (excludes Shrike encounters)
Our Philosophy

Pilgrimage is not merely a journey — it is an embrace of uncertainty, a toast to destiny, and sometimes, a polite sprint away from razor-sharp appendages. At ChronoTravel, we believe even terror should feel first-class.

“We don’t guarantee survival, but we guarantee the story will be unforgettable.”


Company Motto, inscribed at Tau Ceti HQ
